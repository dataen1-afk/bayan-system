**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS**:
-   **Core Functionality**: A full-stack application to manage service contracts.
-   **Workflow**:
    1.  Admin creates a form and sends a public link to a client.
    2.  Client fills out a multi-step wizard form (no login required).
    3.  On submission, the system automatically calculates audit duration based on an Excel logic file.
    4.  Admin reviews the submission and calculation on their dashboard.
    5.  Admin generates a price quote based on the submitted data.
    6.  Client receives a link to the quote and can **approve**, **reject**, or **request modifications**.
    7.  Upon approval, the client is redirected to fill out a Certification Agreement form.
    8.  A submitted agreement becomes a contract, and a PDF can be generated, including a digital signature.
-   **User Roles**: Admin (full access), Client (accesses forms/quotes/agreements via public links).
-   **UI/UX**: Professional UI matching the Bayan Auditing brand, multi-language support (English/Arabic), full RTL layout for Arabic, and a collapsible sidebar.
